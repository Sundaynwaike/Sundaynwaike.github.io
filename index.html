Biography

Sunday Nwaike

GLORIOUS VOICE AMBASSADOR

Sunday Nwaike, professionally known as Glorious Voice Ambassador, is a Nigerian gospel singer, songwriter, worship leader, and gospel minister dedicated to proclaiming the Gospel of Jesus Christ through music. Born on 25 May 1975, he hails from Uturu, Isuikwuato Local Government Area, Abia State, Nigeria.

He began his music ministry in 1997 after receiving a clear calling to use his God-given voice to glorify God and minister to people through worship and gospel music. His songs inspire faith, encourage believers, and lead people into a deeper relationship with Jesus Christ.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To proclaim the Gospel of Jesus Christ through spirit-filled music, leading people into true worship, strengthening believers in their faith, and bringing hope, healing, salvation, and encouragement to people across the world.

Our Vision

To become a globally recognized gospel music ministry whose songs inspire heartfelt worship, transform lives through the power of the Holy Spirit, and bring glory to God while drawing countless souls into a closer relationship with Jesus Christ.
